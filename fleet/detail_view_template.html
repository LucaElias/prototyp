Machine Philosophy

The Porsche Cayman GT4 RS represents the absolute peak of atmospheric mid-engine engineering. By shoehorning the 9,000 RPM heart of the 911 GT3 into the compact chassis, Porsche has created a car that rewards precision and punishes hesitation.

Our specific unit is enhanced with the Manthey Racing KW V4 suspension kit, dialed in for the compressions of the Nordschleife. Rolling on Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 R rubber and Endless MA45B endurance pads, this machine is a laboratory for automotive excellence.
