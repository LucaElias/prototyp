Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting a track tool for the Nürburgring Nordschleife. Professional prep, zero hassle.

How do I book a track session? expand_more
You can book directly through our online fleet portal. Select your car, choose the date (Touristenfahrten or Trackday), and complete the security deposit. Our team will confirm the slot within 2 hours.
What is the cancellation policy? expand_more
Cancellations made 7 days prior to the event are eligible for a full refund. Within 48 hours, we offer a 50% credit for future bookings. In case of track closure due to weather, we reschedule at no extra cost.
Can i rent a car without a booking? expand_more
While we recommend booking in advance to guarantee availability, walk-ins are welcome subject to car availability on the day.
Is it necessary to pay a deposit? expand_more
Yes, a security deposit is required for all rentals to cover the insurance excess. This amount is pre-authorized on your credit card and released after the rental, provided there are no damages.
Can i share a car with my friend? expand_more
Yes, additional drivers can be added to the rental agreement for a small fee, provided they meet the age and license requirements.
Do i need an instructor? expand_more
While not mandatory for experienced drivers, we strongly recommend booking an instructor for your first laps to learn the track safely and maximize your experience.
What rules apply on the track? expand_more
During Touristenfahrten sessions, normal German road traffic laws (StVO) apply. This includes a strict "overtake on the left only" rule. Drifting and timing/racing are strictly prohibited.
Can I drive with no experience? expand_more
Yes, beginners are welcome! We advise starting with a slower car or an instructor session to get accustomed to the track layout and traffic.
Can i take passengers? expand_more
Yes, passengers are allowed (must be at least 12 years old generally). Each passenger needs their own lap ticket, unless you have a season card that covers passengers.
Will the track open if it rains? expand_more
Yes, the track usually remains open in rain, but may close for extreme weather like fog, ice, or snow. Drive carefully and within your limits in wet conditions!
Is insurance included? expand_more
Yes, basic insurance is included, but there is an excess (deductible) that you are liable for in case of an accident. The excess amount depends on the vehicle category.
Why are excess levels so high? expand_more
The excess reflects the high risk associated with driving on a race track. Accidents can be costly, involving not just car damage but also track barriers and recovery fees.
What happens if i crash? expand_more
You are liable for damages up to the maximum excess amount agreed upon. Any costs exceeding the excess are covered by our insurance. You must also pay for track damages (barriers) and recovery.
What if the car breaks down? expand_more
If the breakdown is due to a mechanical failure of the car (not driver error), we will provide a replacement vehicle or refund your unused laps. We maintain our fleet to the highest standards to minimize this risk.
