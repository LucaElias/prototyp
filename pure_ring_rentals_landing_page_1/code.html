Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting a track tool for the Nürburgring Nordschleife. Professional prep, zero hassle.

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a track session? expand_more
You can book directly through our online fleet portal. Select your car, choose the date (Touristenfahrten or Trackday), and complete the security deposit. Our team will confirm the slot within 2 hours.
What is the cancellation policy? expand_more
Cancellations made 7 days prior to the event are eligible for a full refund. Within 48 hours, we offer a 50% credit for future bookings. In case of track closure due to weather, we reschedule at no extra cost.

Insurance & Liability

What does the rental insurance cover? expand_more
Our standard insurance covers the vehicle and third-party liability on track. However, every rental has a fixed deductible (excess) based on the car category (e.g., €5,000 for BMW M2 Comp, €10,000 for Cayman GT4).
Is track barrier damage included? expand_more
No. Damage to circuit infrastructure (Armco barriers, towing fees) is billed directly by the Nürburgring 1927 GmbH & Co. KG and is the driver's responsibility.

Track Rules & Safety

Is a helmet mandatory? expand_more
For Touristenfahrten (Public Driving), helmets are highly recommended but not legally required by the track. For Trackdays, they are mandatory. We provide professional Bell/Stilo helmets with every rental.
Overtaking rules on the Nordschleife expand_more
Overtaking is ONLY allowed on the left. Strictly no overtaking on the right. This is a public road during TF sessions, and standard German road traffic laws (StVO) apply.

Need Help?

Our track support team is available 24/7 to assist with technical queries or urgent booking changes.
